About me

I am a Ph.D. student in Brain, Mind and Computer Science at the University of Padua, Italy. Here, I am part of the Security and PRIvacy Through Zeal (SPRITZ) research group. Under the supervision of Prof. Mauro Conti. At a high level, My research is based on ICN and CCN related studies to integrate them in advanced mobile networks for efficient and secure authentication in mobility management. The goal is to review the existing technologies and identify the current challenges in securing the mobility of a user in advanced mobile networks I've completed Bachelors in Electrical (Telecommunication) Engineering from COMSATS Institute of Information Technology, Pakistan and Masters in Computer Network Engineering HALMSTAD UNIVERSITY, Sweden.
